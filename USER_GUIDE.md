# Sarion Team OS

## Practical User Guide for Team Members

Welcome to Sarion Team OS.

This is the single place where the Sarion team plans work, tracks progress, schedules meetings, and records achievements.

### Golden Rule

If a task, target, meeting, or update is not in Sarion Team OS, it does not officially exist.

---

# Daily Workflow

## Step 1: Start Your Day

When you log in:

1. Open Dashboard

2. Review:

   * Today's Tasks
   * Today's Meetings
   * Weekly Progress
   * Notifications

3. Complete your Daily Check-In

---

# Daily Check-In

Purpose:
Tell the team what you plan to accomplish today.

Go to:

Daily → Check-In

Fill:

### Focus

What is your main goal today?

Example:

* Finish homepage design
* Complete Stripe integration
* Publish LinkedIn content

### Priorities

List your most important tasks.

Example:

* Finalize pricing page
* Review onboarding flow
* Prepare weekly report

### Blockers

Anything preventing progress?

Example:

* Waiting for approval
* Missing assets
* Waiting for feedback

Click:

Save

Do this every morning.

# Working With Tasks

Tasks are how work gets tracked.

Go to:

Tasks

---

## Understanding Task Status

Tasks move through these stages:

### Draft

Task created but not assigned yet.

### Assigned

Task assigned and ready to start.

### In Progress

Currently being worked on.

### In Review

Work completed and waiting for review.

### Approved

Work accepted.

### Completed

Task fully finished.

Always keep task status updated.

---

## Task Priorities

### P0 Critical

Urgent work affecting business operations.

### P1 High

Important work requiring attention soon.

### P2 Medium

Normal planned work.

### P3 Low

Nice-to-have or low urgency work.

# Weekly Review

Complete every Friday.

Go to:

Weekly Review

Fill:

### Completion %

A slider (0–100%) for how much of your weekly plan was completed. Sarion
suggests a starting value based on your weekly targets — adjust as needed.

### Weekly Summary

What was achieved this week (what went well, what didn't, key learnings).

### Carry-forward Tasks

Incomplete work that should roll into next week. Sarion pre-fills a suggestion
you can edit.

Click:

Save review

* Due tasks

* Inside the app

3. Create action items

# Success Rules

1. Update tasks immediately.
2. Never leave completed work unrecorded.
3. Complete Daily Check-In every morning.
4. Complete End Of Day every evening.
5. Every meeting should produce action items.
6. Every action item should become a task.
7. Review weekly progress every Friday.
8. Keep targets updated.
9. Use Sarion Team OS daily.
10. Keep all work visible to the team.
